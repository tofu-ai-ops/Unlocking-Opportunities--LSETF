# Unlocking-opportunities--LSETF

# What it does
LSETF is a digital transformation of the Lagos State Employment Trust Fund (LSETF) — a platform designed to simplify how Lagosians discover, apply for, and manage employment and business support opportunities.
It connects job seekers, entrepreneurs, and small business owners with verified funding, training, and mentorship programs — all in one transparent, trusted space.

# Why does it matter?
Many Lagosians are unaware of the opportunities available through LSETF or find it difficult to navigate the process due to misinformation and fake websites. Unlocking opportunities bridges this gap by making verified government opportunities visible and easily accessible.
By rebuilding trust and simplifying access, the platform empowers citizens to establish sustainable businesses, acquire employable skills, and contribute to the growth of Lagos' economy.

# Who is it for?
Small business owners, Entrepreneurs who are seeking funding and a community of entrepreneurs in their niche
Youths, between the ages of 18 and 35 seeking employment.
Start-ups and Innovators with ideas but no funds to start.

# Key features
Smart Application Portal: End-to-end digital application and review.
Mentorship Hub: Connects beneficiaries with verified mentors.
Impact Dashboard: Real-time tracking of loans, jobs, and training programs.
Trust Layer: Verified government-backed links and updates to prevent scams.
AI Eligibility Advisor: Personalized program recommendations.

Backend: Python, JavaScript, Node.js
